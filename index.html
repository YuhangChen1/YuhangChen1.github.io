Yuhang Chen (陈 宇航)

Yuhang Chen (or Francis, a name from a novel and TV series he really likes, House of Cards) is an apprentice.

He is a Junior student in the School of Computer Science and Technology (Qiming College) at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), Wuhan, China. He is fortunate to be guided by Associate Professor Yao Wan from HUST. He is a member in ONE Lab, where he met his academic mentor, Dongping Chen (PhD student, now at the University of Maryland).

His research interests lie in 3D spatial reconstruction and generation, multimodal foundation models and world models, He is continuing to take his initial steps in scientific research. He is always a learner.
